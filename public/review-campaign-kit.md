1. Go to **https://qr.io** or **https://www.qr-code-generator.com** (both free)
2. Select **"URL"** as the QR code type
3. Paste the review link for the platform you want
4. Click **"Generate"** or **"Create QR Code"**
5. Download as **PNG** (minimum 1000 x 1000 px for print quality)
6. Repeat for each platform (Shiksha, Collegedunia, Google Maps)

Each printed QR poster should include:
- **Heading:** "Share Your JKKN Experience"

## 7. Tips for Writing Helpful Reviews

> **Share this with alumni, students, and faculty before they write their review**

### What Makes a Review Genuinely Useful

Prospective students and parents trust specific, detailed reviews far more than generic star ratings. Here is what to include:

#### Essential Elements (Always Include)
- **Batch year and program** — e.g., "BDS 2018–2023" or "MDS Orthodontics 2021–2023"
- **One specific experience** — a clinical case, a faculty moment, a placement story
- **Honest assessment** — strengths AND what could improve (balanced reviews are more trusted)

#### High-Impact Topics to Cover

| Topic | What to Write About |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Clinical Training** | OPD patient volume, case variety, hands-on exposure from early years |
| **Faculty** | Teaching quality, accessibility, subject expertise, mentorship |
| **Infrastructure** | Number of dental chairs (200+), equipment quality, simulation lab |
| **Hospital Exposure** | 500+ daily OPD patients, multi-specialty teaching hospital, 500+ beds |
| **Placements** | Where you work now, placement cell support, campus recruitment |
| **MDS Specialization** | For MDS graduates — research opportunities, PG clinical training |
| **Campus Life** | Hostel, sports, extracurriculars, student welfare |
| **Location** | Connectivity from Salem, Erode, Namakkal, Coimbatore |

#### What NOT to Write
- Do not copy-paste the same review across platforms (each should be unique)
- Do not write only 1 sentence — aim for at least 4–5 sentences
- Do not fabricate or exaggerate — authentic reviews build long-term trust
- Do not include personal contact info or private information

#### Sample Review Starter (Fill in Your Own Details)
> "I completed my BDS from JKKN Dental College in [YEAR]. The clinical training was one of the biggest strengths — the hospital sees 500+ OPD patients daily, so practical exposure starts from [year]. The faculty in [department] were particularly supportive during [experience]. If I had to suggest an improvement, I would say [honest feedback]. Overall, [your final recommendation]."
