**Q1. What programs does JKKN Dental College & Hospital offer?**

JKKN Dental College & Hospital offers BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) with 100 seats and MDS (Master of Dental Surgery) in five specializations: Periodontics, Orthodontics, Prosthodontics, Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics, and Oral Medicine & Radiology. Both programs are DCI approved and affiliated to Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University.

---

**Q2. Is JKKN Dental College approved by the Dental Council of India (DCI)?**

Yes, JKKN Dental College & Hospital is fully approved by the Dental Council of India (DCI). The college is also NAAC A accredited and affiliated to Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Guindy, Chennai.

---

**Q3. What is the NEET cutoff for BDS admission at JKKN Dental College?**

NEET is mandatory for BDS admission. The cutoff varies each year based on the number of applicants and seat availability. Candidates are advised to check the latest cutoff on our official admissions portal at https://admission.jkkn.ac.in or contact us at +91 9345855001 for the current year's cutoff details.

---

**Q4. How do I apply for BDS admission at JKKN Dental College?**

Applications for BDS admission can be submitted online through our admissions portal at https://admission.jkkn.ac.in. Eligibility: NEET qualified, 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, minimum 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories). For assistance, call +91 9345855001 or email dental@jkkn.ac.in.

---

**Q5. What is the duration of the BDS program?**

The BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) program is a 5-year course, which includes 4 years of academic and clinical training followed by 1 year of compulsory rotatory internship. The program is affiliated to Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University.

---

**Q6. How many dental chairs are available at JKKN Dental Hospital?**

JKKN Dental College & Hospital has 200+ dental chairs spread across multiple clinical departments, providing excellent hands-on training opportunities for students. The hospital serves 500+ patients daily, ensuring rich clinical exposure from the very first year of the BDS program.

---

**Q7. What specializations are available in MDS at JKKN Dental College?**

MDS (Master of Dental Surgery) is offered in five specializations:
1. Periodontics
2. Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics
3. Prosthodontics & Crown & Bridge
4. Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics
5. Oral Medicine & Radiology

All specializations are DCI approved and provide advanced clinical training under experienced faculty.

---

**Q8. Where is JKKN Dental College located?**

JKKN Dental College & Hospital is located at Natarajapuram, NH-544 (Salem-Coimbatore Highway), Komarapalayam, Namakkal District, Tamil Nadu 638183. The campus is easily accessible from Salem (35 km), Erode (45 km), Namakkal (25 km), and Tiruchengode (15 km).

---

**Q9. How far is JKKN Dental College from Salem and Coimbatore?**

JKKN Dental College & Hospital is approximately 35 km from Salem and 130 km from Coimbatore. The campus is situated directly on NH-544 (Salem-Coimbatore Highway), making it easily accessible by road. Nearby cities: Tiruchengode (15 km), Namakkal (25 km), Erode (45 km).

**Q11. Does the college provide transport facilities?**

Yes, JKKN Dental College & Hospital offers college transport services connecting the campus to Salem, Erode, Namakkal, Tiruchengode, and surrounding areas. Students can opt for transport facilities during the admission process. Contact +91 9345855001 for route details.

**Q12. What dental services are available at JKKN Dental Hospital for patients?**

JKKN Dental Hospital provides a wide range of dental services including general dentistry, orthodontic treatment (braces and aligners), periodontal treatment, root canal therapy, dental implants, prosthodontic services (crowns, bridges, dentures), oral surgery, pediatric dentistry, oral medicine and radiology, preventive dentistry, and emergency dental care — all at affordable prices.

### Photo Upload Tips

1. **Cover photo:** Use the best front exterior shot of the college building — this is the first image visitors see.
2. **Logo:** Upload the official JKKN Dental College logo as the profile photo.
3. **File format:** JPG or PNG, minimum 720 × 720 px, maximum 5 MB per photo.
4. **No watermarks:** Avoid uploading photos with competitor watermarks or unrelated branding.
5. **Geo-tag:** If possible, enable location tagging on photos before uploading.
6. **Update frequency:** Add at least 2–3 new photos every month to keep the profile fresh and signal activity to Google.
7. **Patient photos:** Never upload identifiable patient photos without explicit written consent.
